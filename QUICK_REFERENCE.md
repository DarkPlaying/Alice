Phase 0 (Briefing):

strict 60s timer.
Show Rules & Points table.
Auto-advance to Setup.
Phase 1 (Target Selection):

Players: detailed voting mechanism. Players click cards to "Nominate" for Angel/Demon. The cards with the highest votes at the end of the phase become the Player Team's Angel & Demon.
Master: Selects their own Angel and Demon.
Phase 2 (Hunting & Evaluation):

Players: Individually vote to guess the Master's hidden cards.
Master: Votes to guess the Players' hidden cards.
Simultaneous Resolution: When Master clicks "Evaluate", scores are calculated based on cross-matches.
Phase Ends (Cleanup):

Card Removal:
The cards playered by the Master (Angel/Demon) are removed from the Players' View (they can't guess them again).
The cards played by the Players (Angel/Demon) are removed from the Master's View
